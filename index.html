⚠️ Basisprüfung — First Year Examination Block 2

All second-semester courses form part of the Basisprüfung block exam.

📝

CourseDurationLanguageWeightAids

⚠️
Block Rules
Total: 56 ECTS (28 per semester)
Weighted average ≥ 4.0 across ALL first-year courses
Two attempts total — fail = repeat entire block
Fail rate: ~30-40%

Exam session: Calendar weeks 32–35 (Aug 2026)
Repetition: Every session, no re-enrollment needed

💡 Bonus points:
• A&P: Earn bonus via exercises & peer feedback
• Analysis I: Interactive tasks → up to +0.25 grade bonus
• DDCA: Labs count 30% — don't skip them!

Info
Recordings
Labs
Staff
Resources

📅 Schedule

  • Lectures: Thu 14:15–16:00, Fri 14:15–16:00
  • Location: HG F7 (overflow: HG F5)
  • Livestream: YouTube Live
  • Wiki: Course Wiki
  • Moodle: Open Moodle

📚 Topics

  • Boolean algebra & logic gates
  • Transistors & CMOS
  • Combinational & sequential logic
  • Finite state machines
  • Hardware Description Languages (SystemVerilog)
  • ISA & RISC-V
  • Single-cycle & pipelined processors
  • Memory hierarchy & caches
  • Out-of-order execution

📝 Grading

  • 70% Final Exam — written, 180 min, English
  • 30% Labs — mandatory, on-site + reports
  • ⚠️ Not participating in a lab = 0 for that lab
  • Examiners: S. Sadrosadati, O. Mutlu
  • Part of Basisprüfung Block 2

💡 Pro Tips

  • Prof. Mutlu is legendary — engaging lecturer
  • ALL lectures on YouTube (every year!)
  • Start labs EARLY — hardware debugging is slow
  • Watch past years' videos for review
